You can access compute resources in Kaggle Notebooks by adding this competition as an input. NVIDIA Blackwell provides access to an NVIDIA RTX PRO 6000 Blackwell GPU, which you can use as your GPU accelerator.
There are a few key steps to keep in mind:
Develop a strategy: Feature engineering, prompt engineering, and generating chain-of-thought (CoT) traces are commonly used approaches.
Build a Solver and Synthesizer (Optional): Since the competition dataset is relatively small, it may not provide enough signal for training a 30B parameter model. To address this, design a solver and synthesizer to generate a high-quality synthetic dataset.
Train the model: I recommend forking my Submission Demo Notebook (https://www.kaggle.com/code/amith1707/nvidia-nemotron-submission-demo-fixed) and integrating your training code into the appropriate cell.
Wishing you the best of luck in this competition!
