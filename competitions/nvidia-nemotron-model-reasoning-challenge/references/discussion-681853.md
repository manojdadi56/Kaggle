Hii buddy, In most Kaggle competitions, internet access is disabled during submission runs, regardless of the accelerator (like RTX PRO 6000 or H100).
So even if you select a GPU, you usually won’t be able to access external APIs or download data during inference. Everything needed must be included in the notebook or dataset beforehand.
It’s always best to double-check the competition rules page, but in general, assume no internet access during submission. I hope it will help you👍
