Unit testing model on simple bit transformations
I tried unit testing nemotron model on simple transformations:
xor,or,and,shift,rotaion,majority,choice,nand,nor,not,identity
Only one operation was ever applied on the input.
Here is the notebook that vizualizes results: notebook
Even on such basic tasks without finetuning its only correct about 50% of the time.
Most successfull it is on: identity and not, but even on idnentity it managed to fail
So to conclude model doesn't understand well enough basic operations, hence I decided to create dataset, that can be used for training bitwise problem.
